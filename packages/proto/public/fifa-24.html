EA Sports FC 24 (formerly FIFA 24)

The world's game with authentic football experiences, featuring HyperMotionV technology and enhanced realism.

Game Details

Release Date: September 29, 2023

Platform: PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, PC, Nintendo Switch

Genre: Sports Simulation

Developer: EA Vancouver

Publisher: Electronic Arts

Series: FIFA/EA Sports FC

Gameplay Features

  • HyperMotionV technology for realistic movement
  • Ultimate Team with enhanced chemistry
  • Career Mode improvements
  • Volta Football street football
  • Cross-play support
  • Enhanced goalkeeping AI

Game Description:

EA Sports FC 24 brings you closer to football than ever before, powered by three cutting-edge technologies that work together to create the most realistic football experience: HyperMotionV, which captures the rhythm and fluidity of real-world football using volumetric data from 180+ professional matches.

PlayStyles optimised by Opta and other leading data providers recreate the unique characteristics of the world's best players, making them more authentic than ever. And Enhanced Matchday Experience recreates the biggest matches in world football with a broadcast-like atmosphere.

Experience the most authentic football game ever made with EA Sports FC 24. Whether you're building your dream team in Ultimate Team, managing your favorite club in Career Mode, or playing with friends in Volta Football, EA Sports FC 24 delivers the beautiful game like never before.
