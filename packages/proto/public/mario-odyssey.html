Game Description:

Explore incredible places far from the Mushroom Kingdom as you join Mario and his new ally Cappy on a massive, globe-trotting 3D adventure. Use amazing new abilities—like the power to capture and control objects, animals, and enemies—to collect Power Moons so you can power up the Odyssey airship and save Princess Peach from Bowser's wedding plans!

This sandbox-style Mario game returns to the open-ended, exploration-based structure of Super Mario 64 and Super Mario Sunshine, while introducing a new capture mechanic that allows Mario to take control of various characters, enemies, and objects.

Visit astonishing locales, such as the skyscraper-packed New Donk City, and run into some friendly faces there. Thanks to the power of the Nintendo Switch system, Mario's incredible 3D world is now portable, so you can have fun whenever and wherever you are!
